About the Department

The Department of Computer Science offers industry-oriented education in Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing and Full Stack Development. The department provides modern laboratories, experienced faculty, project-based learning and placement assistance to prepare students for successful careers.

Vision

To become a center of excellence in Computer Science education, research and innovation.

Mission

  • Provide quality technical education.
  • Develop innovative and ethical professionals.
  • Promote research and entrepreneurship.
  • Enhance industry collaboration.
